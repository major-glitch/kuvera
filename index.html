The Services I offer

Analysis

Analysis as first step in system development has significant impact on the project's success. Together we accomplish this by delineating the project's scope and by establishing a common basis for communication for all stakeholders involved.

Development

Development of secure, scalable and data-driven web/mobile applications made in Python, HTML5, JavaScript and CSS3. Tailored to the client's needs and budget.

Process Improvement

Processes are used for all types of work, including new product development, production and delivery of goods and services. Using Lean and Six Sigma principles I'll help my clients to conduct a systematic root-cause analysis to discover process improvements aimed at making processes more cost-effective, dependable, responsive and flexible.

Data Engineering

At it’s core, data engineering is a form of communication. It is my goal to enable my clients to walk away from my reports and infographics with insight and understanding. To tell stories, spark discussion, and create calls to actions.

Hello, I am Kristof Gillisjans...

I am a versatile freelance Analyst and Data Engineer with over 15 years of various experiences in complex projects in the following domains: pharmaceutical research, ICT, mail sorting, logistics, retail, supply chain, EMS dispatching, energy, banking and HR.
I specialize in process improvement, data analysis and web technologies.
I have strong interpersonal skills, combining result orientation with coaching capabilities.
